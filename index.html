MtMtEye-app Info

The More than Meets the Eye MtMtEye-app is pinpointing Wikipedia-generated (local) references on Google Maps and Google Street View, and then generating links to thousands of related images in UB Leiden"s Special Collections. It doesn’t matter where you are in the world; browsing maps or Street View will take you to the Leiden catalogue with its rich collections.

Better still, the same code can be used by any library or museum so they, too, can provide easy links to their collections without any hassle.

  • * Now: link to UB special collections (Leiden local catalogue).
  • * Extras, to be developed:
    • - pins in Streetview with own LOGO or brand;
    • - second and third links to images in Flickr, Google;
    • - more links to collections of 071-members
  • * Future: link to other collections (to be adapted by institution which wants to use our code)
The MtMtEye-app is developed by the UBL Hackaton ‘Team DiscoverSC’.
Former professional photographer Giulio Menna is holding a MA in Book and Digital Media Studies (Leiden) and using his coding skills to show the world the beauty of Leiden’s special collections.

Codirector of Sexy Codicology Marjolein de Vos, MA in English and Book and digital media studies, is now a Van der Sande junior fellow, and famous for developing the crowdsourced Digitized Medieval Manuscripts Maps website.
