MtMtEye-app Info

The 'More than Meets the Eye' or MtMtEye-app is linking to thousands of images from UB Leiden's famous Special Collections as you roam around Google's Street View, anywhere in the world.
Each pin gives you the link to relevant images inside the Special Collections, which can be browsed for free. The MtMtEye-app is developed by the UBL Hackathon Team 2.
